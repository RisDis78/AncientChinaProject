What is this website about?

My social science teacher assigned everybody a project called the final inquiry project(FIP).
So for the assignment I am doing a whole website.
The main idea of the project is how trade affects culture?
Throughout this website I will explain how, geography, economy, and history affect trade.
I am also going to feature some other fun stuff to look at that relates to the topic.
I hope you enjoy your journey throughout this website.

Claim

Trade does affect culture.
Many things go hand and hand where it comes into play.
There are many things to consider. For an example in today’s culture.
Many people don’t eat meat as part of their culture.
So the more vegetarian restaurants pop up the better demographic they have.
It is smart for them because the more vegetarians they’re are the more popular they get.
Now they could buy bulks of food which gives them a discount.
Now as more and more people come. The place gets more popular and even non-vegetarians come to eat.
